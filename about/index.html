About Us

Founded in Spring 2017, CampusGo is the brain child of Brittany Andrews. Brittany, a native of RVA, is a graduate of John Marshall High School. Brittany holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Comprehensive Music Education from Delaware State University and is currently pursuing her Master's Degree in Nonprofit Studies at the University of Richmond. Brittany's passion for helping young people realize their college dreams comes from working with students for most of her professional career. After visiting over 30 college campuses, she believes that it only takes one visit to know that you are home.

Vision

At CampusGo it is our hope to offer young people the opportunity to visit a broad range of college campuses. As the organization grows, it is our wish to partner with other local college access organizations, youth groups, and school to provide students the best opportunity to realize their college dreams.
